S. Liu received the M.S. degree in computer software and theory and the Ph.D. degree in earth exploration and information technology from Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China, in 2017 and 2024, respectively. Prior to that, he was a senior engineer at four AI companies from 2017 to 2021 respectively.

From 2024 to 2025, he was a Visiting Ph.D. Student with the Faculty of ITC, University of Twente, Enschede, Netherlands. He is currently a Lecturer with the School of Computer Engineering, Chengdu Technological University, Chengdu, China.

His research interests lie in machine learning and image processing.
