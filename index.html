Shujun Liu received the BS degree from Yibin University, Yibin, China, in 2014, and the MS degree from Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China, in 2017. He is currently a PhD candidate in the College of Geophysics at Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China. His current research interests include optimal transport, convex optimization, manifold learning, speech recognition and speech synthesis.
