Shujun Liu received the BS degree from Yibin University, Yibin, China, in 2014, and the MS degree from Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China, in 2017. He is currently a PhD candidate in the College of Geophysics at Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China. Before that, he was a speech algorithm engineer at Chengdu Tuya Technology Co., Ltd, Ping An Technology Co., Ltd, Bitman Corp. and Sichuan Changhong AI Lab(CHAIR) respectively from 2017 to 2021. His current research interests include optimal transport, convex optimization, manifold learning, speech recognition and speech synthesis.
