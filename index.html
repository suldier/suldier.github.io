He is currently a Ph.D. candidate in Earth Exploration and Information Technology at Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China, where he received the M.S. degree in Computer Software and Theory, in 2017. In 2014, he got the B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Yibin University, Yibin, China. From 2017 to 2021, he worked as a speech algorithm engineer with the Chengdu Tuya Technology Co., Ltd, Bitman Corp., Ping An Technology Co., Ltd, and Changhong AI Lab (CHAIR). His current research interests lie in optimal transport theory, speech synthesis, and hyperspectral image processing.
