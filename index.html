He is currently a Ph.D. candidate in geophysical prospecting and information technology at Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China. Before that, he received the B.S. degree from Yibin University, Yibin, China, in 2014, and the M.S. degree from Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China, in 2017. From 2017 to 2021, he was a speech algorithm engineer at Chengdu Tuya Technology Co., Ltd, Bitman Corp., Ping An Technology Co., Ltd, and Sichuan Changhong AI Lab (CHAIR) respectively. His current research interests include optimal transport, convex optimization, manifold learning, speech recognition and speech synthesis.
