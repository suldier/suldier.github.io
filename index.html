He received the M.S. degree in computer software and theory from Chengdu University of Technology, Chengdu, China, in 2017, where he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in earth exploration and information technology.

From 2024, he was a Visiting Ph.D. Student with the Faculty of ITC, University of Twente, Enschede, The Netherlands. His current research interests lie in machine learning and image processing.
